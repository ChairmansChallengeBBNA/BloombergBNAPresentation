2018 Diversity & Inclusion Chairmen's Challenge

To align with the overall diversity initiatives promoted at all Bloomberg businesses and subsidiaries, Bloomberg BNA looked to our existing strengths to demonstrate that inclusion has always been a part of the Bloomberg BNA culture.

Diversity & Inclusion Introduction

Diversity Video

This video, which uses several members of the Bloomberg BNA ‘family,’ is structured like a visual tone poem, lyrically expressing who we are as a company, why diversity is important to us, and how being your authentic self is important—both for employees and for the company itself—looking for a variety of backgrounds and perspectives to inform our products and services.

Pick a word

What diversity means to you.

At the annual company picnic, we invited employees to write a word on a vast canvas, which illustrates “what diversity means to you.” We then invited select employees to explain their words in more detail, and why Bloomberg BNA is an ideal, multicultural workforce.

Cultural Months

For nearly every month of the year, Bloomberg BNA linked cultural celebrations relevant to that month to our employees, inviting them to share their experiences in video format. These videos, which feature candid interviews with our diverse workforce, are promoted online and inside the main corporate office.
